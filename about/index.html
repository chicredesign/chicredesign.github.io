Céline Riard

Principal Designer

Céline founded CHIC Redesign in 2003.

Born and raised in Normandy (France) in a picturesque coastal village, she considers her designs like French cuisine: the use of simple flavorsome ingredients with minimal processing, in small portions but bursting with character.

Trained as an environmental engineer from one of the most prestigious colleges in France, she is very practical and a born problem solver.

Her creations are a balanced mix of energy and comfort, the results of years of research and observation on the effects of a positive environment on people's general well-being.

She loves to share her passion for interior redesign, as a design instructor in Keefe Tech Continuing Education program, and as a columnist for the Metrowest Daily News.

Her diverse clientele* shows that Celine and her team are excellent at translating ideas no matter what their clients' cultural heritage may be. (* CHIC clients' countries of origin in alphabetical order: Canada, China, Cuba, Egypt, France, Germany, India, Iran, Israel, Italy, Japan, Lebanon, Netherlands, Nigeria, Pakistan, Portugal, Russia, Uganda, USA, Vietnam)

Jonette Mamba

Assistant & Junior Interior Designer

Jonette is an honors graduate from The New England Institute of Art (NEIA) with a Bachelor’s of Science in Interior Design.

Prior to joining CHIC Redesign, she was an outstanding student and the NEIA Interior Design Peer Tutor for her entire program. She interned at Drummey Rosane Anderson Architects in Waltham, MA. Jonette has excellent general interior design education experience and specializes in computer 3D drawing and floor plans.

She is a native of the Philippines, and grew up in Queens, New York before moving to Milford, CT then to Massachusetts. She has a Cochlear Implant that forces her to see and hear the world differently.

Fortunately for CHIC Redesign, it has also helped her demonstrate that she is an avid problem solver, dedicated learner, and a very determined achiever.

Jonette loves the thrill of adventures and travel and aims to bring her multi-cultural experiences to the profession of Interior Design.

Michelle Murphy

Interior Design Consultant

Michelle graduated Magna Cum Laude from the University of New Haven in 2014 with a Bachelor of Arts in Interior Design.

Throughout her education, she was an avid member of the American Society of Interior Designers and student rep for the Connecticut Coalition of Interior Designers while also volunteering her little spare time to many local charities and organizations.

Her previous design experience includes interning and working for two design firms, PNB Interior Design Inc. and Barbara Block Interiors.

Michelle started at CHIC Redesign as an intern before becoming an Interior Design Assistant and later an Interior Designer. Her strong organization and time management skills make her a great asset for CHIC. Michelle currently works as an Interior Design Consultant for CHIC as she travels the world and tries new things.
